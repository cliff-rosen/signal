BotBeam

Tell your AI what to show. It shows up.

BotBeam gives your AI a set of browser tabs it can push content to. Research a person, draft an email, pull up a webpage — each one appears in its own tab, in real time. Works with ChatGPT and Claude.

Example conversation
"Pull up John's LinkedIn in the research tab and draft a reach-out email in the outreach tab"
Done — I've loaded John's LinkedIn profile in research and written a personalized intro email in outreach. Both tabs are live now.
More things you can say:
"Summarize this article and show it in the notes tab"
"Show the company's homepage in the research tab"
"Put a pros/cons list in the comparison tab"
